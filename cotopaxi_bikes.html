Cotopaxi National Park tour is an exciting day that begins with spectacular views of the Andean landscape in the Avenue of the Volcanoes. Once inside the National Park you will hike to José Rivas Refuge (4.864 m), and maybe even to the glacier if the weather is favorable (5.100 m). During the hike you will have the opportunity to discover the amazing flora inside the reserve. Once at the refuge you will hike back to the parking lot and then have the opportunity to bike downhill to the largest lagoon of the park, Limpiopungo Lagoon. This will be about 18 Km of biking to enjoy the landscape. Then you will go have a nice lunch, followed by the return to Quito.
