Itinerary

Itinerary - Day 1

  • Quito - Lago Agrio - El Puente. Flight (9:00.) or bus ride (one day before - 8 hour ride) to Lago Agrio. Drive from Lago Agrio to El Puente
  • Afternoon: canoe trip down the Cuyabeno River to our camp site.
  • Dinner and exciting night walk.

Day 2

  • Three hour hike in the primary forest to learn about flora and fauna, including lessons in the use of plants for survival. Lunch at Lodge, siesta time.

  • Chocolate activity, homemade chocolate from cocoa beans.

  • Relax sunset at Laguna Grande with a chance for some refreshing swimming and caiman (alligator) searching.

Day 3

  • An hour and a half boat ride down the river to a Siona indigenous community to interact and learn about their traditions and customs by making some local food. After a visit to a shaman to learn about his secrets and ancestral knowledge of shamanism.

  • We enjoy watching hundreds of different insects coming to special lamps connected to solar system to attract them.

Day 4

  • El Puente - Lago Agrio - Quito. Early morning: Bird watching from a 25 meters high tower

  • Breakfast: journey up Cuyabeno River to El Puente.

  • Drive to Lago Agrio (arrive in Lago Agrio in the afternoon).

  • Flight or bus ride back to Quito.

Cuyabeno and its tour of 4 days and 3 nights is promoting seriously and responsibly ecotourism in the 2nd biggest protected area in the Amazon Rainforest of Ecuador (CUYABENO WILDLIFE RESERVE) with 604,000 hectares of primary forest declared protected in 1979. This Ecolodge provides solar electricity to charge batteries of cameras or other electronic objects, rooms with private toilet and hot shower, to make as les impact is possible to nature the lodge also provides bio digestors for the toilet system. Sure the sustainable tourism industry is benefiting directly local communities in the reserve, at the same time protecting a part of the last refuge of wildlife on earth.
