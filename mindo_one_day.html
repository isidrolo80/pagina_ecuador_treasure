Itinerary

ITINERARY:

  • Pick up
  • Departure from Restaurant “Q” (Reina Victoria y Foch)
  • Tarabita on the canopy of the forest and a walk to the waterfall
  • There are two activities to choose from, Zip Lining crossing part of the forest or tubbing down the river with rapids.
  • Visit to a local hacienda to observe hummingbirds, butterflies and orchids inside the property.
  • Lunch at a local restaurant in Mindo
  • Visit to the chocolate factory "YUMBOS" and you can taste the delicious chocolate
  • Return to Quito.

Get to the cloud forest of Ecuador. Located northwest of Quito you will find Mindo, the hummingbird’s homeland. Here, you will find many activities to do such as Canopy, with 13 zip lines of different lengths up to 400 m (1312 ft) with different characteristics for tourists of all ages. Tubing down a local river with rapids. If you prefer a more relaxing activity, then a hike across the cloud forest to visit the waterfalls of Mindo is the perfect option for you. In the afternoon, you may choose between visiting the chocolate factory to learn the process of the elaboration of chocolate, or the butterflies and hummingbirds farms. (Note: All times are approximate, EXCEPT starting time.)
